What We Do

We are OAUTHC Paediatric Unit, created by the blue moravians society in 2010.We are a polygot medical society that cover a lot of services for different customers, but our area of deep specialization is in pediatrics and gyneacology. We also offer emergency services to all our customers and this certainly includes first time customers. Our services are efficient and we pride in the good health of our customers.

How We Work

We work 24 hours a day and 7 days a week, Emergency services are also available round the clock. We have 6 doctors whose combined specializations cover a very wide area of the health care system.Cunsultation hours and are very flexible to suit the schedule of our clients and patients.Medical Counselling services are also available and We have a tested and proven commitment to keeping our services at par with the requirements of our clients.
